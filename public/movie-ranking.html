The Best Movies According to Joyce

My top 3 movies of all time.

Spirited Away

The soundtrack is gorgeous! I was lucky enough to see a Studio Ghibli live concert conducted by Joe Hisaishi.

My Neighbor Totoro

My comfort movie, I first saw this as an elementary school student in China.

Howl's Moving Castle

It was difficult choosing between this and Princess Mononoke! But I love Howl and Sophie, and Calcifer is amazing.
